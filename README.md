# GIS Portfolio — Josh Melleuish

## About Me

I am an environmental scientist with a background spanning research, environmental health, and regulatory compliance, currently upskilling in GIS and actively seeking to pivot into a spatial analysis or GIS-focused role in the environmental sector.

I hold a Bachelor of Environmental Science (Honours) from the University of Wollongong (2022), where my Honours research involved geochemical dataset analysis using Python and R. My professional experience includes a role as an Environmental Health Officer at Shoalhaven City Council, where I integrated environmental monitoring data into a public-facing GIS platform and used spatial analysis to support development approval processes.

I am self-directed in my GIS development, working through practical QGIS projects to build competency across the full geospatial workflow — from sourcing and managing spatial datasets through to constraint analysis, suitability modelling, and professional map production. The projects in this portfolio are designed to reflect real-world tasks in the environmental and renewables sectors, and to demonstrate the skills I am building toward a career in spatial analysis.
